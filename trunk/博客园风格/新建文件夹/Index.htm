After a long winter that refuses to go away, we here at Myclean are super excited for summer! It will be nice to go outside again and actually want to be there! But with summer in NYC comes humidity – our least favorite weather phenomena by far – and with humidity comes mold. Lots and lots of mold.

This might be a little gross, but on those days when the humidity reaches a point where all you want to do is sit inside with the AC blasting, mold spores are everywhere. And if you’re not careful, your apartment may be an unwitting host to an awesome mold party, and everyone’s going to be there! Cladosporium, Penicillium, Aspergillus, and Alternaria will all be attending, not to mention a lot of other hangers-on whose names we can’t pronounce. And if you’re unlucky, the coolest mold of all will be there: Stachybotrys Chartarum, or Stachy for short. Stachy doesn’t usually show up, but it’s not exactly rare that he makes an appearance either – especially in a city environment – and he brings all kinds of fun with him.

天锁斩月

几年的工作中,经历了2个几十号人以上的大项目.深深体会了,一个好的框架对项目的成成败是多么重要的. 尤其是我上一个项目.做的是一个国内顶尖的医疗公司的一个门户项目.当时由于项目的时间比较紧,没有过多时间去考虑和研究框架.于是就简单引进公司的另外一个框架,到最后的2年多使用时间,就逐渐感觉到了那框架的弊端.到后面项目中的很多同事都反映,该框架不但没有提高效率,而且严重阻碍项目的进度.结果也恰恰证明了这一点.使得我们中的很多开发进度,都是严重推迟了.
